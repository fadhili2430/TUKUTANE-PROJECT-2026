About Tukutane

Tukutane is a dynamic social event management platform designed specifically for university students. Whether you're looking to organize a study group, social gathering, or campus-wide event, Tukutane makes it easy to connect with your community and create memorable experiences.

Our Mission

To empower students by providing a seamless platform to discover, organize and participate in social events that foster community, friendship and unforgettable campus experiences.

Our Vision

To be the go-to platform where every student can find their community, build connections, and create lasting memories through impactful social events.
